About Me

Hi, my name is Patrick Mwila. I currently serve as the ICT Innovations & Administrator at the Information and Communications Technology Association of Zambia (ICTAZ). In this role, I lead efforts to coordinate IT infrastructure, implement solutions tailored to organizational needs, and provide consultancy on technological tools that enhance the efficiency of end-to-end operations and events.

Beyond my professional responsibilities, I enjoy blogging about computing technology, covering the work I do—including teaching, research, and community engagement—as well as occasional reflections on my other interests.

In addition to my work in technology, I have a strong interest in financial literacy, investments, entrepreneurship, and, more recently, effective nutrition and healthful living.
